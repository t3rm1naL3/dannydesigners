We are a young Architectural Company founded in 2013. Danny Group of Designers is a creative group of architects with a wide

range of development studies and many completed studies and many completed projects.

Such a varied range of projects follows the inner philosophy and emphasizes the variety of tasks.

The foundation of our profession is the focus on the quality of living. We believe that a perfect, detailed design, creativity and emphasis on the

aesthetics are the main things which give our buildings kindness.

To us, design is not just fancy, but apart from aesthetic demands, should link together functionality and cost-effectiveness.

We think about design and as well as the needs of client who are going to live in it.

Advanced and timeless design is included in all our projects. We prefer perfect design which does not make cold and impersonal

impression at the same time.
